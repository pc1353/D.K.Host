D.K.Enterprise is a leading manufacturer of precision CPVC and UPVC brass inserts, Established in 1982 by Mr. Durlabhjibhai K. Chaniyara.

We are manufacturer of all kind of precision machined components in India. By the years of experience and broad vision of its dedicated and enthusiastic executives and managerial staff, the company could achieve its own name in the industries of Brass Products.

D.K.Enterprise is spread across 6000 Square feet working space in GIDC Phase I, Jamnagar, Gujarat, India with warehouse facilities.

We are here to manufacturing high standard products as per customer's specifications. We always work with high qualified & experienced quality control department. We warmly welcome all business inquiries & assure our best attention & cooperation.
